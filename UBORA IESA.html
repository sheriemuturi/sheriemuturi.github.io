Our Products

UBORA IESA

Loans are given against member’s savings (shares) subject to a maximum of 2.5 times amount of shares and must be fully guaranteed by active IESA members. One must be a member of Ubora Sacco Society Ltd. Minimum monthly savings is Ksh. 200.00. Interest on deposits is 5% p.a. on the closing balance computed monthly and is posted to members’ accounts twice a year (June and December). Part-withdrawal of deposits is allowed when there is no running holiday loan.

  1. Main /Development Loan

    1. Maximum loan amount is Ksh.3million
    2. Maximum repayment period is 48 months
    3. Interest is charged at 14.4% p.a or 1.2 % pm on reducing balance

  2. Main Loan Bridging

    1. Maximum loan amount is Ksh.3 million
    2. Maximum repayment period is 48 months
    3. Interest is charged at 12% p.a or 1 % p.m
    4. One-off interest is charged on outstanding loan at 5%.
    5. One-off processing fee is charged at ksh.500.00

  3. Emergency Loans

    1. Maximum loan amount is Ksh. 100,000
    2. Maximum repayment period is 12 months
    3. Interest is charged at 14.4% p.a or 1.2 % pm on reducing balance

  4. Emergency Loan Bridging

    1. Maximum loan amount is Ksh. 100,000
    2. Maximum repayment period is 12 months
    3. Interest is charged at 14.4% p.a or 1.2 % pm
    4. One-off interest is charged on outstanding loan at 5%.
    5. One-off processing fee is charged at ksh.500.00

  5. Second IESA Emergency loan

    1. Maximum loan amount is Ksh. 30,000
    2. Maximum repayment period is 12 months
    3. Interest is charged at 18% p.a or 1.5 % pm on reducing balance

  6. Compounded / flat rate loan

    1. Maximum loan amount is Ksh. 3 million
    2. Monthly repayment is at a fixed amount comprising of both principal and compounded interest
    3. Interest rate is charged at 13% per annum
    4. Maximum repayment period is 48 months
