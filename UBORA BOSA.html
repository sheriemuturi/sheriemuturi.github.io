Our Products

UBORA BOSA

Loans are given against member’s savings (Deposits) subject to a maximum of 3 times amount of savings. Loans must be fully guaranteed by active Sacco members. Minimum monthly deposits (savings) contribution is 3% of basic salary or Ksh. 1,200 whichever is higher. Interest is paid at the end of year based on net earnings and subject to approval in the Annual General Meeting (AGM). The following products are available:

Types of Loans

  1. Main /Development Loan

    1. Maximum loan amount is Ksh.4million
    2. Maximum repayment period is 60 months
    3. Interest is charged at 12% p.a or 1 % p.m
    4. Repayment is computed on a monthly flat rate for the repayment period.

  2. Main Loan Bridging

    1. Maximum loan amount is Ksh.4million
    2. Maximum repayment period is 60 months
    3. Interest is charged at 12% p.a or 1 % p.m
    4. One-off interest is charged on outstanding loan at 5%.
    5. One-off processing fee is charged at ksh.500.00

  3. Education Loans

    1. School fees loan
      • Maximum loan amount is pegged to ability to repay
      • Maximum repayment period is one calender year
      • Interest is charged at 14.4% p.a or 1.2 % pm
    2. College fees loan
      • Maximum loan amount is pegged to ability to repay
      • Maximum repayment period is 24 months
      • Interest is charged at 14.4% p.a or 1.2 % pm

  4. Emergency Loan

    1. Maximum loan amount is Ksh. 100,000
    2. Maximum repayment period is 12 months
    3. Interest is charged at 14.4% p.a or 1.2 % pm

  5. Emergency Loan Bridging

    1. Maximum loan amount is Ksh. 100,000
    2. Maximum repayment period is 12 months
    3. Interest is charged at 14.4% p.a or 1.2 % pm
    4. One-off interest is charged on outstanding loan at 5%.
    5. One-off processing fees is charged at ksh.500.00

  6. Business Loan

    1. Maximum loan amount is Ksh.800,000 subject to a maximum of 3 times amount of shares for all outstanding loans
    2. Maximum repayment period is 48 months
    3. Interest is charged at 14.4% p.a or 1.2 % pm
    4. Applicants are required to provide their certified bank statements for the latest 3 months
    5. Loan repayment is through direct transfer of funds from applicant’s bank account to Ubora Sacco bank account, upon filling the Direct Debit Authority Forms. Guarantors are required to sign on a separate form confirming that they are in agreement with this arrangement.
