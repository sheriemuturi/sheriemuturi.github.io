About Us

Ubora Cooperative Savings and Credit Society was registered on 4th April 1979. The registered office of the Society is located within the premises of Kenya Bureau of Standards along Popo Road, Off Mombasa Road in Nairobi South C.

Savings

Minimum monthly deposits (savings) contribution is 3% of basic salary or Ksh. 1,200 whichever is higher. Interest is paid at the end of year based on net earnings and subject to approval in the Annual General Meeting (AGM)

UBORA Welfare Scheme

Aims and Objectives: To assist members in meeting burial expenses for themselves and their immediate family members.

Membership: Principle membership to the scheme shall be voluntary and one must be a member of Ubora SACCO to qualify for joining.

Benefits: A sum of 30,000/= (thirty thousand shillings only) shall be paid in event of the death of the principle contributor or any of the listed next of kin subject to the terms and conditions of the insurance company.
