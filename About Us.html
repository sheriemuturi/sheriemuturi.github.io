About Us

BACKGROUND

Ubora Cooperative Savings and Credit Society was registered on 4th April 1979. The registered office of the Society is located within the premises of Kenya Bureau of Standards along Popo Road, Off Mombasa Road in Nairobi South C. Taking cognizance of the very competitive, challenging and dynamic financial environment, the Society over the years has continued on the success trajectory as reflected by financial performance, membership growth, loans, member deposits, turnover.

Membership consists of the following:
  1. Staff of the Kenya Bureau of Standards;
  2. Staff of the Kenya Accreditation Service;
  3. Former employees of the two organizations.

VISION

To be the Sacco of choice in Kenya

MISSION

Ubora Sacco exists to mobilize funds and offer credit to empower members economically by providing quality financial services in collaboration with our stakeholders

MOTTO

Together we prosper
